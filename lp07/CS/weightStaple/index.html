Weight Stapling

In this event, athletes will compete to see how can staple the thickest packet of paper using a standard-issued stapler. Should the staple not protrude through the back of the last sheet of paper, the athlete will be disqualified. This is certainly a risky event, as there are no second chances. One staple--that's all each athlete gets. For this event, there are weight brackets by gender:

  • 39 to 42 kg (86 to 92 lb)
  • 46 kg (101 lb)
  • 50 kg (110 lb)
  • 54 kg (119 lb)
  • 58 kg (128 lb)
  • 63 kg (139 lb)
  • 69 kg (152 lb)
  • 76 kg (167 lb)
  • 85 kg (187 lb)
  • 85 to 100 kg (187 to 220 lb)
